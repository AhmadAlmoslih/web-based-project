Check out Abu Dhabi

Explore diverse nature. Abu Dhabi's landscapes transform dramatically as you venture inland from the sea. Along the 700km coastline, you'll discover a maritime culture, golden beaches and glistening waters hugged in places by giant mangrove forests.

  • Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque
  • Ferrari World
  • Emirates Palace
  • The Louvre Abu Dhabi
  • Emirates Park Zoo

Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque

The Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque is located in Abu Dhabi, the capital city of the U.A.E. The largest mosque in the country, it is the key place of worship for daily prayers. A smaller replica of the grand mosque exists in the Indonesian city of Surakarta.

Ferrari World

Ferrari World Abu Dhabi is a mostly indoors theme park located on Yas Island in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. It is the world's first Ferrari-themed park and features Formula Rossa, the world's fastest roller coaster. The foundation stone for the park was laid on 3 November 2007.

Emirates Palace

The Emirates Palace Mandarin Oriental, Abu Dhabi is a luxury five-star hotel in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. It has been operated by Mandarin Oriental as of 1 January 2020.

The Louvre Abu Dhabi

The Louvre Abu Dhabi is an art museum located on Saadiyat Island in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. It runs under an agreement between the UAE and France, signed in March 2007, that allows it to use the Louvre's name until 2037, and has been described by the Louvre as "France's largest cultural project abroad.

Emirates Park Zoo

Emirates Park Zoo houses over 1700 creatures along with hosting over 250 rare and endangered species. With the Emirates Park Zoo tickets get a chance to explore the various sections of the zoo such as Primate Parade, Petting Zoo and Wildlife Walk. Enjoy the amazing shows such as the Lion Presentation show, Tropical Birds show and others. This zoo is a perfect destination to have a fun day with your friends and family with a wide range of animal areas and activities to enjoy.
